Outdoor Activites

Greece is distinguished by its ancient history, whose features and presence are still clearly visible today, and the natural places and vital places that attract tourists from different parts of the world, and perhaps the most prominent of them are:

Meteora Monastery

Meteora Monastery is very quiet and full of spirituality, with an impressive architecture.

Ruins of delphi

A tour of the ruins of Delphi in the low slopes of Mount Parnassus, and a look at the Theater of Epidaurus (this Greek architectural masterpiece in a small village)

A walk in the Acropolis, this huge edifice of marble stones,It located in Athens

Shipwreck Bay

Shipwreck Bay on the island of Zakynthos, which has an unparalleled landscape, is one of the most beautiful islands in Greece and the most beautiful beaches in the world.
