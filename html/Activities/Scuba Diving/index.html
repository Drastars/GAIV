Bubblemaker

Bubblemaker is as fun as it sounds. Children who are at least 8 years old can use scuba gear to breathe underwater and swim around in shallow water.

Freediver

Free diving is breath-hold diving. Without the assistance of a breathing apparatus you will learn to control your breathing and you will go deeper, longer, further in the underwater world.

Hundreds of miles of coastline and numerous islands close to Athens and Attica, Greece is a developing scuba dive destination with many followers from all over the world.

Another great spot for scuba divers is found off Schinaria Beach, in the north of Crete. This small area includes habitat for black-fish, octopus, moray eels, and many other marine species.

Corfu has some of the best diving sites in the Ionian Sea!

There are plenty of things to explore in the island of Santorini, including diverse and fascinating marine life, as well as underwater sea caves and a ship wreck which divers can explore.
